# Team Onboarder

> Register any team's database into Talk2Data so they can query their data using plain English — no SQL knowledge required.

---

## What Is This?

Team Onboarder is the setup step for Talk2Data. Before a team can start asking questions like *"Who are the top performers this quarter?"* or *"Show me open headcount by department"*, their data needs to be registered.

This app does that registration in minutes — no engineering work, no schema expertise required.

---

## Who Is This For?

- **Team leads and data owners** who want to make their team's data accessible via chat
- **Platform/data teams** onboarding multiple business units into Talk2Data
- Anyone who manages data but doesn't want to write SQL or configure pipelines

---

## What Does It Do?

### Connects to Your Database
Point it at any database your team uses — it works across all major database platforms. You provide the connection details; the app handles the rest.

### Understands Your Data Automatically
The app reads your database structure and uses AI to suggest a name and description for your team's data domain. You review and refine these before anything is saved.

### You Choose What to Expose
Select which tables and columns are relevant for your team. You stay in control of what data the chat app can see and query.

### Builds a Clean, Queryable Layer
Behind the scenes, the app creates a curated view of your selected data — resolving any naming conflicts automatically so the chat app always gets clean, unambiguous results.

### Registers Your Team Instantly
Once confirmed, your team's data is indexed and immediately available in the Talk2Data chat app under their namespace. No waiting, no deployment.

---

## How Long Does It Take?

For a typical team with a well-understood database, onboarding takes **under 5 minutes**:

1. Open the app
2. Connect to your database
3. Confirm the AI-suggested name and description
4. Pick your tables and columns
5. Click **Build & Index**

Done — your team can now chat with their data.

---

## What Happens After Onboarding?

The Talk2Data chat app picks up your team's namespace automatically. Users on that team can immediately start asking natural language questions against the data you selected — no further setup needed.

Each team's data remains isolated under their own namespace, so there is no risk of one team seeing another team's data.
